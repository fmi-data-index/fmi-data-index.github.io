PedX

http://pedx.io/

Overview

PedX is a large-scale multi-modal collection of pedestrians at complex urban intersections. The dataset provides high-resolution stereo images and LiDAR data with manual 2D and automatic 3D annotations. The data was captured using two pairs of stereo cameras and four Velodyne LiDAR sensors. You can find out more about data acquisition and annotation methodologies here

Associated Paper or Article

For more information please read PedX: Benchmark Dataset for Metric 3D Pose Estimation of Pedestrians in Complex Urban Intersections.

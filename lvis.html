LVIS

https://www.lvisdataset.org/

Overview

The LVIS dataset contains the same image data as the COCO dataset. For more details on that you can access our info page on the COCO dataset.

In particular, LVIS presents over 1200 categories and over 2 million high quality segmentation masks, as well as a broad selection of "rare categories".

Associated Paper or Article

For more information please read LVIS: A Dataset for Large Vocabulary Instance Segmentation.

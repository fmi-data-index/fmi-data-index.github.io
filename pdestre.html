P-DESTRE

http://p-destre.di.ubi.pt/index.html

Overview

P-DESTRE is a multi-session dataset of videos of pedestrians in outdoor public environments, fully annotated at the frame level by providing one text file for each video file.

The data has been acquired via drones that were flying at between 5.5 and 6.7 meters height, with the camera pitch angles varying between 45º to 90º. Volunteers were mostly in the 18-24 age interval (> 90%), roughly divided into two halves for gender (approx 65% males), and ethnicity (”white” and ”Indian”). About 28% of the volunteers were using glasses, 10% of these were using sunglasses. Data were recorded at 30fps, with 4K spatial resolution (3,840 × 2,160), and stored in ”mp4” format, with H.264 compression.

The P-DESTRE datset has been contructed to help accomplish 4 tasks: pedestrian identification, pedestrian tracking, pedestrial re-identification and pedestrian search.

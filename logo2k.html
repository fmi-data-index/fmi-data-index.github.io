Logo+2k

https://github.com/msn199959/Logo-2k-plus-Dataset

Overview

Logo-2K+ covers a diverse range of logo classes from real-world logo images, containing 167,140 images with 10 root categories and 2,341 categories.

Associated Paper or Article

For more information please read Logo-2K+: A Large-Scale Logo Dataset for Scalable Logo Classification.

Annotations

Annotations consist of simple image labels. Labels are stored in .txt files that contain the image paths and are part of the paths.
